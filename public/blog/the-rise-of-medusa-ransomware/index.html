What Is Medusa Ransomware?#

Medusa is a ransomware-as-a-service (RaaS) group that became prominent in 2023 and continues to evolve. Unlike traditional ransomware, Medusa is designed to disable security software, exfiltrate sensitive data, and encrypt files across infected systems.

It commonly gains access via:

  • Phishing emails (often disguised as Microsoft or Google services)
  • Unpatched RDP or VPN services
  • Compromised login credentials

Once inside, it can:

  • Move laterally across networks
  • Exfiltrate confidential files
  • Encrypt local and network files
  • Demand ransom with threats of public leaks
